Applied Random Forest, LASSO, and multinomial logistic regression to model household debt levels across OECD economies. Random Forest outperformed multinomial logistic regression (90.5% vs. 76.9% accuracy), identifying credit market accessibility and income inequality as the dominant predictors of household debt.
