Cleaned and standardized large-scale bibliometric records on the eugenics movement (1880-1945), refining a domain-specific lexicon to structure inconsistent historical source data. Built an R pipeline calling the Gemini API to disambiguate and correct author and source records at scale, designing blocking and pairwise-comparison routines for unstructured data.
