Group project with Pengzhen Wang and Wenqing Zhang (NYU, "Applied Statistics and Econometrics"). Led all coding, data cleaning, and empirical strategy: estimated survey-weighted difference-in-differences and event-study models on CPS ASEC microdata, and combined seemingly unrelated regression (SUR) models to jointly test significance across coverage, financial protection, and health outcomes.
